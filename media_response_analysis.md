## 🔍 深度分析

### 中文分析

**核心发现：**

特朗普面对媒体批评时展现出典型的**"攻击性防御"**模式——这与他"绝不示弱"的人设高度一致。

**防御链条：**

```
第1步：否认 (Denial)
       ↓ "我没说过那样的话" / "那是假新闻"
第2步：攻击来源 (Attack the Source)
       ↓ "记者是骗子" / "媒体是人民公敌"
第3步：反向指控 (Counter-accusation)
       ↓ "你们才应该被调查" / "他们才是坏人"
第4步：受害叙事 (Victimhood Narrative)
       ↓ "只有我在说实话" / "所有人都在针对我"
```

**心理动机：**

1. **维护完美自我形象**：任何批评都被视为对"完美自我"的威胁
2. **控制叙事**：将讨论焦点从批评内容转移到"谁在说"
3. **情绪主导**：用愤怒替代理性回应，避免深入讨论
4. **团结支持者**：通过"我们 vs 他们"叙事强化基本盘

**潜在意图：**

这种模式不仅仅是防御，更是一种**进攻策略**——通过持续攻击来削弱对手的可信度，同时让支持者对主流媒体产生免疫。

### English Analysis

**Core Finding:**

Trump's typical response to media criticism demonstrates a classic **"Aggressive Defense"** pattern, highly consistent with his "never show weakness" persona.

**Defense Chain:**

```
Step 1: Denial
        ↓ "I never said that" / "That's fake news"
Step 2: Attack the Source
        ↓ "The reporter is a liar" / "The media is the enemy of the people"
Step 3: Counter-accusation
        ↓ "You should be investigated" / "They're the real criminals"
Step 4: Victimhood Narrative
        ↓ "I'm the only one telling the truth" / "Everyone is against me"
```

**Psychological Motivation:**

1. **Self-Preservation of Grandiose Self-Image**: Any criticism is perceived as a threat to his "perfect self"
2. **Narrative Control**: Shifts focus from the criticism itself to "who's speaking"
3. **Emotional Dominance**: Uses anger over reason to avoid substantive discussion
4. **Base Mobilization**: Reinforces "us vs them" narrative to solidify supporter loyalty

**Underlying Intent:**

This pattern is not merely defensive—it's an **offensive strategy**. By continuously attacking, he weakens opponents' credibility while building supporter immunity to mainstream media.
